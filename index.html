EGLC uses a steep 5.5¬∞ approach. Low visibility and specific "minima" (minimum visibility allowed to land) are critical here. Look at this real-world complex METAR:

EGLC 150920Z 26012KT 0400 R27/0600D FG VV001 05/05 Q0998
